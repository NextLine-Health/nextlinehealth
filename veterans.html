Why This Matters

Veteran behavioral health is not only about access. It is about continuity.

Resources exist, but many veterans still fall through the cracks after the first referral, discharge, intake, or crisis touchpoint.

NextLine Health helps organizations close that gap through structured outreach, care navigation, accountability, and human follow-through.
